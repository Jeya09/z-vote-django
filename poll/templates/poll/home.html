What is Blockchain based E-Voting?

Blockchain based voting is a secure, transparent and decentralised way of voting. It taps the secure blockchain technology, essentially converting ballots into transactions and securely mining blocks out of them. This way anyone can monitor votes but at the same time your vote is completely anonymous, preserving your identity.
